Shan Yichun

Shan Yichun (Chinese: 单依纯), born on December 23, 2001, in Dongyang, Zhejiang Province, China, is a rising Chinese singer-songwriter celebrated for her pure tone and emotional depth. She began showing musical talent at a young age and later studied at the Zhejiang Conservatory of Music, where she refined her vocal and performance skills. Shan first gained national attention after winning the 2020 season of Sing! China, mentored by renowned artist Li Jian. Her soulful performances of songs like “It’s You” and “You Are the April of This World” captivated both audiences and judges, marking her as one of the most promising young vocalists in China.

Following her victory, Shan released several well-received singles and collaborated on various soundtracks, earning praise for her delicate yet powerful voice and sincere interpretation of lyrics. She has been featured in prominent media outlets, including Billboard China, and continues to explore a mix of pop, soul, and R&B influences in her music. Known for her humble personality and artistic sensitivity, Shan Yichun represents a new generation of Chinese artists who bridge emotional authenticity with contemporary musical expression.
